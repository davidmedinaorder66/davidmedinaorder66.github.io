How did the celebration come about and why May 4? It is recorded that the first celebration of Star Wars Day was in Toronto, back in 2011,
when a fan event was organized with activities alluding to the saga,
from trivia, costume contest and the screening of the first films
The reason why it is celebrated exactly on May 4 is linked to one of its
most famous quotes: May The Force Be With You, fans decided to set the special day on May 4,
the wordplay and pronunciation in English, which is May the 4th (fourth)
